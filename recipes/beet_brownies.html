Beet Brownies

4 large eggs
2 cups sugar
2 medium beets, cut into small cubes
8 ounces melted butter or canola oil
1 cup cocoa powder, sifted
3 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 cup flour of your choice
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Place the beets is a small sauce pan with water just barely covering. Bring to a boil and simmer until beets are very tender, about 40 minutes. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup of the cooking liquid. Puree the beets and cooking liquid in a food processor. You should have about 1 cup beet puree.

Beat the eggs till fluffy. Mix in sugar, then mix in other ingredients in the order listed, beating until smooth. Spread batter into a greased 9x13 pan and bake a 300 for 45 minutes. Cool 1 hour before cutting. Makes 12.
