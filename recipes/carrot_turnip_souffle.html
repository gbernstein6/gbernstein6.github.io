Carrot and turnip souffle

Carrot and turnip are great in combination, but this recipe can be used with either vegetable, or any root vegetable, by itself as well.

1 lb. carrots, chopped
1 lb. turnips, peeled and chopped
1 cup milk
1/2 cup flour of your choice
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 tbs. butter
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
2 eggs, beaten
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
pinch cayenne pepper

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place the carrots and turnips in a saucepan with water to cover. Bring to a boil a simmer until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain, and place in a food processor.
Add the butter, milk and eggs, and blend until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and stir in first the flour, salt, pepper and cayenne, and then the grated cheese and onion. Pour the souffl√© into a buttered 2-quart baking dish or glass pie plate, and bake for 40-45 minutes, until puffed up and lightly golden. Serves 4.
