
1 family pack of chicken thighs
red onion
shallots
bell pepper
chipotle pepper
balsamic vinegar

Saute up onion and shallots to get initial caramelization. Add in chopped up chipotle pepper, pitted cherries and balsamic vinegar, mix up and cover long enough to make sure balsamic vinegar is partially absorbed into cherries. Transfer to slow cooker with chicken and bell pepper. Cook on low.
