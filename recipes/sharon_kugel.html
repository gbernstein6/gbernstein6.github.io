Sharon's Noodle Kugel

Ingredients

1 lb medium width egg noodles
1 lb cottage or pot cheese
1 lb farmer Cheese broken into pieces
1 cup sugar
5 large eggs lightly beaten
2 cups sour cream
1/4 t salt
1 cup whole milk
1 cup golden raisins
1 t vanilla extract
1/2 c unsalted butter
1/4 t ground cinnamon

Procedure

Preheat oven to 350
Cook noodles according to package instructions and drain
In a large mixing bowl combine all ingredients except noodles, butter and cinnamon
Add noodles
Place butter in large baking dish (15.5x10x2.5) and put dish in oven until butter is melted
Pour mixture into pan
Sprinkle cinnamon over the top
Bake for one hour
Cool before cutting
