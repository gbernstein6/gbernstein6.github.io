Ethan's Full Sour Pickles

Makes 30 full sour pickle halves. Fits in 1 liter(?) jar.

Note: Use sea salt or pickling salt (sans iodine/anti-caking agent)
Note: Use filtered/bottled water

For the Brine
3 cups water
2 cups white vinegar
1 tbsp salt
1 tsp sugar

For the Pickles
15(?) small Kirby cucumbers
Dill
Garlic
Peppercorn
Mustard seeds
Chili peppers (optional)

Directions
1. Cut off cucumber tips, then halve.
2. Salt heavily ("It's impossible to over salt!"), let sit 10-15 minutes, then wipe off salt and pat dry.
3. Combine brine ingredients in pot, bring to simmer, then let fully cool to room temp.
4. Place flavorings at bottom of jar, pack cucumber halves on top.
5. Pour in brine, making sure cucumber halves don't bob above the liquid level.
6. Seal and let sit in fridge for 1 week.
