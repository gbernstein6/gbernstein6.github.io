Ingredients


4 cups AP flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp salt
1 small glass tube rum extract
1 small glass tube almond extract (for these two I use Dr. Oetker's extracts, which come in glass vials.
1/2 tsp lemon extract
1/2 tsp. cardamom
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
2 eggs
1 and 1/2 sticks butter
8 oz. cottage cheese or farmers cheese
1 cup currants
1 cup golden raisins
4 oz chopped almonds
2 oz chopped citron (have used dried lemon and/or dried orange too)

Recipe:


By hand or in stand mixer on slow
Sift flour and baking powder together
Make a well in the center and fill with the eggs, sugar, spices and extracts
Mix with some of the flour to create a paste
Add cold butter, cottage cheese, raisin, currants, almonds & citron then knead into a smooth dough
Add flour if its sticky
Form into a loaf and place on a double greased sheet of parchment paper on a baking sheet
Bake at 325 for an hour (or until golden brown)
Brush with melted butter and top with powdered sugar.
