Beef Seaweed Soup

Ingredients
30g dried seaweed, broken into small pieces
0.5 tbsp sesame oil
3 garlic cloves, grated
200-300g marinating beef, sliced into thin 1-inch slices
1 tbsp soup soy sauce (국간장)

Directions
1. Submerge seaweed in cool water, set aside to rehydrate.
2. Heat sesame oil in Dutch oven over medium-low heat. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
3. Add beef and soy sauce. Stir to mix, spread out, and cook until bottom of beef is brown.
4. Drain seaweed and squeeze out excess water. Add to Dutch oven with pinch of salt and a few grinds of black pepper. Increase heat to medium and cook until beef is brown on both sides.
5. Add 1.75L/7.5 cups water, cover, and bring to boil. Reduce to simmer for 5-10 minutes.
6. Season with salt to taste. Serve with rice, kimchi, and Korean pickled pepper.
