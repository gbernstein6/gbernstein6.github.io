Pleskow Charoset

1/2 cup pecan or walnut

3-4 tbsp sugar, decreased to taste
2 medium apples (fuji or honey crisp), peeled, cored
2-3 tbsp lemon juice
1 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 cup almonds, chopped
12 pitted dates, chopped
1/4 cup pine nuts (optional)

1. Combine pecans/walnuts and lesser amount of suger. Grind in processor until fine.
2. Grate half the apples; chop the other half. Mix in lemon juice.
3. Stir in rest of the ingredients.
4. Add more sugar, lemon juice, salt to taste.
