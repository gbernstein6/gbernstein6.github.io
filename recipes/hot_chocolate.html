Jacques Torres' Famous Hot Chocolate with Whipped Cream

Makes 4 3/4-cup servings

Ingredients

3 cups whole milk
1/2 cup (50 g) milk powder
1 tsp (3 g) cornstarch
1 cup (150 g) 60% dark chocolate

Procedure

Bring milk to a boil in a medium sauce pot over medium-high heat.
Lower heat to medium then add chocolate, whisking vigorously until the chocolate is completely melted.
Add milk powder and cornstarch, still whisking, until everything is dissolved and the mixture is smooth and thick.
