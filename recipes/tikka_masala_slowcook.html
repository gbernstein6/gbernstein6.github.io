Slow Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala

3 lbs. boneless skinless chicken thighs (breasts too watery?)
1 large yellow onion, finely diced
5 cloves garlic, minced
4 Tbsp freshly, finely grated ginger
1 (29 oz) can tomato puree or sauce (sub some out for paste)
2 cups plain yogurt (double to make creamier)
2 Tbsp lemon juice
4 Tbsp Garam Masala
2 Tbsp cumin
1 Tbsp paprika
2 tsp salt, or to taste
3/4 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 tsp cayenne pepper
2-3 tsp fenugreek leaves

In a large mixing bowl combine chopped onions, minced garlic, grated ginger, tomato puree, plain yogurt, lemon juice, Garam Masala, cumin, paprika, salt, cinnamon, pepper, cayenne pepper, and fenugreek. Stir until combined. Pour half of sauce mixture into a large slow cooker then add in chicken. Then cover chicken with remaining sauce. Cover slow cooker and cook on low heat for 8 hours (or high for 4 hours).
